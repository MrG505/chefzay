Chef Zay

Private Chef • Local Grass-Fed Steak Specialist • Albuquerque, NM

Precision. Craft. Performance.

Chef Zay delivers elevated, animal-based private dining experiences rooted in clean, high-protein whole foods. With years of hands-on culinary dedication and formal training, every plate emphasizes local sourcing and exacting technique.

Specializing in sous vide, flame-grilled, butter-basted grass-fed steaks from Sweet Mercy Farms, No Bull Prime Meats, MAS Cattle Company, and Nelson Meats. Fresh Rainbow Trout finished with signature lemon pepper butter. Fruits and approved produce sourced from Albuquerque farmers markets and organic suppliers.

Philosophy

Menus follow nose-to-tail principles with premium animal proteins, organs, raw dairy, fruit, and honey. All preparation honors the Animal-Based Guide for flavor, texture, and performance.
