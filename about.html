Chef Zay

Private Chef • Steak Specialist • New Mexico

Mastery Rooted in Craft

Chef Zay brings over two decades of hands-on culinary dedication to every plate. With formal culinary arts training and a relentless focus on precision, he has refined his signature approach: elevated, steak-forward cuisine grounded in clean, high-protein ingredients and scratch cooking.

Specializing in dry-aged ribeyes, premium wagyu, and reimagined viral favorites, Chef Zay creates unforgettable private dining experiences tailored for discerning clients in Albuquerque, Rio Rancho, Corrales, and across New Mexico. His work blends technical excellence with a deep understanding of performance nutrition — delivering flavor and results.

Philosophy

Every menu reflects a commitment to whole-food excellence and culinary performance. Inspired by animal-based nutrition principles, Chef Zay crafts meals that honor tradition while meeting the demands of modern, active lifestyles.
