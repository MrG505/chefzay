About ChefZay

Real cooking. Real ingredients. Real passion.

My Background

I’ve been cooking since I was 5 years old. I studied culinary arts for 3 years, worked in fast food management, and have spent years cooking for myself, friends, and family.

My Specialty

Steak is my passion. I focus on replicating and improving popular dishes from scratch using real ingredients and strong cooking technique.

My Philosophy

I believe in real food, cooked properly, with quality ingredients and respect for nutrition. My cooking is inspired by an animal-based, whole-food approach.

My Goal

I’m building private chef services to support my independence, create stability, and eventually work toward owning a home and land in New Mexico.
