About Me

My name is Isaiah Gallegos. I’ve been cooking since I was 5 years old, learning from my mother, family, friends, and years of hands-on experience.

I specialize in natural, real-food cooking made from scratch whenever possible. My focus is premium steaks, New Mexico-inspired dishes, and custom meals built around high-quality ingredients.

I believe great food starts with real ingredients, and I prioritize local, natural, and organic products whenever available.
