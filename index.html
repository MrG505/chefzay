About Me

My name is Isaiah Gallegos. I’ve been cooking since I was 5 years old, learning from my mother, family, and years of hands-on experience.

I specialize in natural, real-food cooking made from scratch whenever possible. My focus is premium steaks, New Mexico-inspired dishes, and custom meals built around high-quality ingredients.

Every meal is prepared with care, attention to detail, and respect for quality ingredients.

My Cooking Philosophy

My approach to food is simple: real ingredients, minimal processing, and bold natural flavor.

I focus on high-quality proteins like grass-fed beef, pasture-raised poultry, eggs, and seafood, combined with fresh fruits, natural fats, and seasonal ingredients.

I prioritize locally sourced, organic, and clean ingredients whenever possible, creating meals that are both nourishing and satisfying.

The goal is simple: food that tastes amazing and makes you feel amazing.

Where I Source My Ingredients

I prioritize sourcing ingredients from trusted local farms, ranches, and butchers in New Mexico.

Farms & ranches:

• Sweet Mercy Farms — 100% grass-fed and finished beef, local shares & bulk boxes
• MAS Cattle Company LLC — ranch-direct bulk beef
• JX Ranch — grass-fed/finished beef from regional ranch operations

Trusted local butcher:

• Nelson’s Butchery (Albuquerque) — quality meats, fresh cuts, and specialty orders

This network allows me to cook with clean, high-quality ingredients and stay connected to local New Mexico food culture.
